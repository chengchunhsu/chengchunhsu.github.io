# About me

* I am a Research Assistant in [Vision and Learning Lab](http://vllab.cs.nctu.edu.tw/) at [National Chiao Tung University](https://www.cs.nctu.edu.tw/?locale=en), advised by Prof. [Yen-Yu Lin](https://sites.google.com/site/yylinweb/). [[Curriculum Vitae](https://chengchunhsu.github.io/files/CV_ChengChun_Hsu.pdf)] <!--[[Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Ixg9n-EAAAAJ&hl=en)]-->
* My research focuses on computer vision and perception of machines. Particularly, my work aims to enable the machines to learn to understand visual data and improve its recognition ability with fewer annotation efforts.
* I am interested in **Instance Segmentation**, **Domain Adaptation**, and **Weakly-supervised Learning**.
* I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology in 2019.
