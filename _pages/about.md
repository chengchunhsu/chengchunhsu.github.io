# About me

* I am a Research Assistant in [Vision and Learning Lab](http://vllab.cs.nctu.edu.tw/) at National Chiao Tung University, advised by [Prof. Yen-Yu Lin](https://sites.google.com/site/yylinweb/). I am also working intensively with [Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang](https://faculty.ucmerced.edu/mhyang/) (University of California, Merced) and [Dr. Yi-Hsuan Tsai](https://sites.google.com/site/yihsuantsai) (NEC Laboratories America). [[Curriculum Vitae](https://chengchunhsu.github.io/files/CV_ChengChun_Hsu.pdf)] <!--[[Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Ixg9n-EAAAAJ&hl=en)]-->
* My research focuses on computer vision and perception of machines. Particularly, my work aims to enable the machines to learn to understand visual data and improve its recognition ability with less human supervision.
* I am interested in **Instance Segmentation**, **Domain Adaptation**, and **Weakly Supervised Learning**.
* I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology in 2019.
